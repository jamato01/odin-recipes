Bang Bang Chicken Bites Recipe

Description

These bang bang chicken bites are spicy, crispy little chicken nuggets, air fried or baked, then tossed in a quick chili sauce.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need to make this easy chicken bites recipe:

  • 24 ounces frozen crispy chicken nuggets
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha hot sauce, plus more if desired
  • ¼ cup sliced green onions

Steps

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe for these delicious chicken bites below:

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 300° F (193° C)
  2. Add nuggets in a single layer to the air fryer basket, and fry until hot and crisp, about 10 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through cooking time. You may need to work in batches. (Alternately, preheat the oven to 400° F. Place nuggets in a single layer on a baking sheet, and bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 12 minutes.)
  3. Meanwhile, stir mayonnaise, chili sauce, and hot sauce together in a large bowl until well blended. Toss hot chicken nuggets in sauce until well coated. Top with green onions before serving.
